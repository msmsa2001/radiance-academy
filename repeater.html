Course Overview

The NEET Repeater Course is specifically tailored for students who have attempted the NEET exam but wish to enhance their scores for better college admissions. This intensive course focuses on rigorous preparation to ensure success in the NEET exam.

Eligibility/Duration/Subjects

> Students who have previously attempted the NEET exam.

Student progress is closely monitored through regular tests, practice exams, and mock NEET examinations to gauge their readiness for the actual NEET exam retake.
